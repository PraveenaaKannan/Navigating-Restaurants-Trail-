Restaurants face several key challenges in managing operations effectively. They often lack insight into what dishes are popular, when the busiest hours are, and how frequently customers return. This absence of data-driven decisions leads to inefficiencies in service, staffing, and customer satisfaction.

Our project, Navigating Restaurant Trail, aims to solve these problems using an interactive and intelligent dashboard built with IBM Cognos Analytics. By analyzing customer behavior, dining patterns, and sales data, we transform raw information into actionable insights for restaurant managers.

  • Identifies peak dining hours to better allocate staff and resources
  • Tracks customer loyalty to retain high-value customers
  • Analyzes menu performance to highlight top and low-selling dishes
  • Displays KPIs like revenue, order volume, and feedback scores
  • Uses heatmaps and graphs to visualize traffic and table usage

Restaurants collect large volumes of data every day‚Äîfrom food orders and payment types to customer visits and staff schedules. However, without proper tools, this data remains unused. Our dashboard simplifies this complexity by organizing and presenting insights visually, making it easier for restaurant owners to:

  • Make faster and smarter business decisions
  • Improve the overall dining experience
  • Manage inventory and reduce waste
  • Offer personalized promotions to loyal customers
  1. Data Collection: Creating a rich dataset with 40 attributes (orders, items, customer feedback, staff, payments, etc.)
  2. Data Preparation: Cleaning and modeling data in IBM Cognos Data Modules
  3. Dashboard Design: Building visuals like bar charts, pie charts, KPIs, and heatmaps
  4. Analysis: Discovering customer habits, peak times, and top dishes
  5. Optimization Suggestions: Recommending ways to improve operations based on insights
  6. Sharing Results: Exporting or sharing dashboards to guide real-time decisions

In conclusion, our project delivers a modern, data-driven solution to the restaurant industry by unlocking insights hidden in daily operations. With this tool, Perfect Plate and other restaurants can boost efficiency, revenue, and customer satisfaction through smarter analytics.
